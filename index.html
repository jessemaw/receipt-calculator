How to Use This Receipt Rewards Calculator

This free receipt rewards calculator helps you estimate how much money you can earn by scanning your everyday shopping receipts. Simply adjust the sliders to match your shopping habits — how many receipts you scan per day, your preferred categories, and how long you plan to use the app — and instantly see your projected earnings.

Comparing Receipt Apps: Crinkl vs Fetch vs ReceiptPal

Not all receipt scanning apps pay the same. Our calculator compares the three most popular receipt rewards apps that pay you for any receipt:

  • Crinkl — Pays $0.10 per receipt with up to 3x category multipliers and $CRINKL token holder bonuses. Daily limit of 10 receipts (70/week). Learn more →
  • Fetch Rewards — Pays $0.025 per receipt. Simple to use but caps at 35 receipts per week.
  • ReceiptPal — Pays about $0.06 per receipt. Accepts any receipt type but limits to 12 per week.

What Affects Your Receipt Scanning Earnings?

Your total earnings from receipt apps depend on several factors: number of receipts scanned (more shopping = more rewards), receipt categories (groceries and dining often pay bonuses on Crinkl), consistency (daily streaks and weekly bonuses add up), and app-specific features like referral bonuses or token holdings. Our calculator factors in all of these variables for each app.

Is Scanning Receipts Worth It?

For most people, receipt scanning apps are worth the minimal effort. If you scan just 3 receipts per day, you could earn $50-$300+ per year depending on the app. The key is choosing the right app for your habits and staying consistent.
